Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our platform, process, or cross-border logistics? We’ve got answers.

How long does an auction run?

Auctions on Earth Metals typically run for 5-10 business days, giving qualified buyers sufficient time to review inspection reports and place competitive bids. Specific auction durations are set per asset.

How are assets inspected before listing?

Every asset undergoes a professional third-party inspection before it is listed. This includes a full condition report, photographic documentation, service history verification, and any known defects clearly disclosed.

What countries do you operate in?

We are headquartered in South Africa and Zimbabwe, with operations serving the wider Southern African region including Botswana, Zambia, Mozambique, and Namibia. Our buyer and seller network spans 6+ countries.

Is Earth Metals open to individual sellers?

Earth Metals is a business-to-business (B2B) platform. Participants must be registered businesses. This ensures all buyers and sellers are qualified, vetted, and capable counterparties - protecting every transaction.

How are payments handled?

Payment is confirmed and secured before any asset handover takes place. Our structured process includes formal documentation at every stage, eliminating risk for both buyer and seller.

How do I register?

Click "Register" on the platform. You will be guided through our business verification process. Once approved, you will have immediate access to browse current auctions or list your first asset - typically within 48 hours.

Our team is ready to walk you through the platform, answer any queries, and help you get started.
